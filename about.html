About Our Dealership

Trizzyy German Auto Hub is a dealership located in Gaborone at 11556 Kgaladua Road. The dealership was established in 2026 with the goal of bringing reliable German vehicles to the Botswana market. The dealership mainly sells Volkswagen vehicles because they are well known for durability, performance and modern technology. Customers can explore models, compare prices and view vehicle images before purchasing.
