About Our Dealership

Trizzyy German Auto Hub was established in 2026 in Gaborone, Botswana. The dealership specialises in selling and marketing Volkswagen vehicles.

The company focuses on providing reliable German engineering vehicles to customers who want quality, performance and safety.

Our showroom located at 11556 Kgaladua Road allows customers to view different Volkswagen models before purchasing.
