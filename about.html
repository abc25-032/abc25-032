About Our Dealership

Trizzyy German Auto Hub is a modern Volkswagen dealership located in Gaborone at 11556 Kgaladua Road. The dealership was established in 2026 to provide customers with access to premium German vehicles.

Our dealership specialises in selling and marketing Volkswagen cars including the Volkswagen Polo, Golf, Passat and Tiguan. These vehicles are known for their performance, safety and reliability.

We aim to deliver excellent customer service while helping clients find vehicles that match their needs and lifestyle.
