About Our Dealership

Trizzyy German Auto Hub was founded in 2026 with the goal of creating a trusted dealership specializing in German vehicles. Located in Gaborone at 11556 Kgaladua Road, the dealership provides quality vehicles and professional services to customers across Botswana.

Our dealership focuses mainly on Volkswagen vehicles because they are known for durability, performance and modern design. Over the years Volkswagen has built a strong reputation globally, and our dealership brings these trusted vehicles closer to customers in Botswana.

Our vision is to become one of the leading German vehicle dealerships in Southern Africa while maintaining excellent customer service and competitive pricing.
