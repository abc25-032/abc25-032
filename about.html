About Our Dealership

Trizzyy German Auto Hub is a locally owned dealership based in Gaborone, located at 11556 Kgaladua Road. Established in 2026, the dealership focuses on selling and marketing high-quality German vehicles, especially Volkswagen models.

We are committed to providing excellent customer service and ensuring that every customer finds a vehicle that suits their needs. Our dealership has quickly become one of the fastest growing car hubs in the area.

Our mission is to deliver reliable vehicles, affordable pricing, and a professional experience to all our customers.
